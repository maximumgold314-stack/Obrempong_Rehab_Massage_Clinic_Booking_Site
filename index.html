Frequently asked questions

Do I need to book in advance?
It's recommended, especially around midday when the clinic tends to be busiest. Walk-ins are welcome when a therapist is available — calling ahead is the surest way to get your preferred time.
Which treatment is right for me if I've had a stroke?
Our stroke rehab massage is designed specifically for post-stroke mobility support. Mention your condition when booking so we can prepare the right session for you.
What should I wear or bring?
Comfortable, loose clothing is best. If you're coming for a medical referral or have specific mobility needs, feel free to bring any relevant notes from your doctor.
